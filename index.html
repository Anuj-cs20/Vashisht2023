ROBO WARS

Teams must bring their own robots.
A 1v1 battle between various teams is
arranged, and the reward will be awarded
to the top three finishers.

MAZE ROVER

Teams are expected to reach the mentioned goal
in a simulated maze with an automatic rover or by
teleoperation with the help of the given clues.
The team with the highest points will be given a prize.

SAE

QUADSKI

Team's are expected to build a remotely
controlled amphibious vehicle that can race on the
track as well as water with other team vehicles.
The team with the least lap time will be awarded.
Slot needed 12th feb 2nd half from 3pm.

CS
CLUB

Capture The Flag

A Capture the Flag event where participants/teams find
flags/specific strings hidden within a webpage, file, etc.
without any hints or instructions which requires
them to explore and utilize their various skills in
scripting, Linux, image manipulation, cryptography,
pen. Testing, etc.

GDSC
CLUB

IIITDM Hacks

A machine learning model-making contest where the
participating teams will have access to a real-world
dataset for which a problem statement will be provided
and the groups/individuals will have to make a predictive
model be capable of predicting results for the
test dataset given. Any algorithm
can be used based on the problem presented.

EPIC
CLUB

Pitch on Pitch

Pitch on Pitch provides a splendid opportunity for upcoming
student entrepreneurs and aspiring startups to pitch their ideas
in front of esteemed juries and create a holistic network with
the investors. It aims to stimulate young minds to think
creatively and work towards innovative business ideas and in
turn contribute to the Indian economy.
